Meet Pop's

The Story Behind the Ragu

Food Is Love.

Pop's makes everything from scratch. The mozzarella is fresh. The marinara is homemade. The meatballs are hand-rolled. Carmine Spinelli brought real Italian cooking to Rocky Point, and people keep coming back.

Before Big Ragu, there was nowhere like this in Puerto Penasco. Now there is. Authentic Italian food, a full pub bar, football on TV, and tables that fill up because nobody wants to leave. Carmine's philosophy is simple: Food is Love. The wall says the rest.
